About Hess Flooring

Our Story

Founded with a commitment to quality craftsmanship and personal service, Hess Flooring has been proudly serving homeowners and businesses throughout the region for over a decade. What began as a small, family-owned operation has grown into a trusted name in flooring installation, known for reliability, honesty, and attention to detail.

We specialize in the sales and installation of laminate, hardwood, carpet, and tile flooring. Whether you're updating a single room or outfitting an entire home or commercial space, we provide tailored solutions to match your style, budget, and performance needs. From start to finish, our team ensures a smooth, stress-free experience with expert guidance at every step.

Why Choose Us

At Hess Flooring, we believe flooring is more than just a surface—it’s the foundation of your space. That’s why we focus on high-quality materials, skilled workmanship, and customer satisfaction. Our team treats every project as if it were our own home, and we take pride in delivering results that last.

Serving the Community

As a locally owned business, we take great pride in contributing to our community and building lasting relationships with our customers. We’re honored to have earned the trust of so many local families and businesses, and we look forward to helping you bring your flooring vision to life.
